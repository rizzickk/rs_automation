Use cases

Examples of what we can automate

Small businesses usually do not need a giant software rollout. They need targeted systems that remove friction from everyday work.

Reporting automation

Weekly or monthly reporting

Automate file pulls, checks, calculations, and final output so your team stops rebuilding the same report by hand.

Problem Manual report preparation takes hours and is easy to break.
Solution Build a repeatable workflow that ingests files and produces output automatically.
Result Less manual work, fewer errors, faster turnaround.
Lead intake

Qualification and routing tools

Use a lightweight decision engine to score incoming leads, flag bad fits, and route stronger opportunities more quickly.

Problem Leads arrive inconsistently and are handled case by case.
Solution Form-based intake with business rules and lead scoring logic.
Result Cleaner pipeline and faster response.
Spreadsheet cleanup

Messy Excel workflows

When multiple spreadsheets feed one process, we can centralize the logic and remove repetitive copy-paste work.

Problem Too many files, tabs, formulas, and manual checks.
Solution Move repeated steps into structured automation.
Result More control and fewer hidden errors.
Operational decision engines

Rule-based business decisions

If your team keeps making the same type of judgment repeatedly, that logic can often be turned into a repeatable system.

Problem Different people apply rules differently.
Solution Build scoring logic, thresholds, and exception handling into one flow.
Result Faster, more consistent decisions.
