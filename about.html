About the Founder

Ricardo Ramirez Jr.

I spent years as a senior analyst inside USAA — one of the largest financial companies in the country. I built models, automated reports, and showed leadership exactly what was happening with their numbers. I got good at seeing things in data that most people don't know to look for.

Then I looked at the businesses in my own community — here in El Paso — and realized most of them have never had anyone do that for them. A construction company working hard for years, not knowing which jobs were actually making money. A church with no real visibility into where donations were going. A nonprofit trying to explain their finances to a donor without a clear picture themselves.

These aren't failing businesses. They're just businesses that haven't had anyone stop and look at the picture with them. That's what I do now.
