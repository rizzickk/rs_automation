SMS Lead Notification Consent

This page explains how SMS notifications are used within tools built by RS Automation EP.

1. Product Description

Lead Scoring AI is a software tool used by real estate agents to receive operational SMS notifications when a buyer submits a qualification form. The tool evaluates buyer readiness and generates a structured report for the agent.

2. Who Receives Text Messages

SMS messages are sent only to registered real estate agents who provide their phone number during onboarding or configuration of the platform.

3. How Consent Is Obtained

By signing up for the platform and providing their phone number, agents consent to receive automated SMS notifications related to buyer lead submissions and readiness reports generated by the system.

4. Message Content

Messages contain buyer lead information and a link to the generated readiness report. These are operational notifications designed to alert agents to new buyer submissions.

5. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies depending on lead activity and the number of buyer submissions received by the agent.

6. Opt-Out and Support

Agents may opt out of SMS notifications by contacting support at ricardo@rsautomationep.com. Future versions of the system may support replying STOP to unsubscribe.

7. Privacy Notice

Information submitted through buyer qualification forms is used solely for generating readiness reports and notifying the assigned agent. Personal information is not sold or shared outside the intended operational workflow.
